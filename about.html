Chauhans Hotel in Mumbai

Chauhans Hotel is a chain of luxury hotels and a subsidiary of the Indian Hotels Company Limited, headquartered in Mumbai, India. Incorporated by Tushar Kumar Singh in 2003

The company is a part of the Tata Group. The company employed over 20,000 people in the year 2012. The company has been ranked as the World’s Strongest Hotel Brand in 2022 and India’s Strongest Brand as per Brand Finance Hotels 50 Report 2020 and India 100 Report 2022, 2023 and 2024.
